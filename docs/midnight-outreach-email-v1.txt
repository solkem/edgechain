Subject: Evidence-Led Midnight Use-Case Pilot in Odzi, Zimbabwe

Hello Midnight team,

We are building an evidence-first field pilot with a smallholder farming cooperative in Odzi, Zimbabwe to validate a practical privacy use-case on Midnight.

This is intentionally not a DeFi lending pitch at this stage.

We are self-funding an initial 6-week traction phase and collecting structured adoption evidence (activation, retention, trust, data quality, anchoring integrity, wallet readiness in an optional champion lane).

1) share the pilot design;
