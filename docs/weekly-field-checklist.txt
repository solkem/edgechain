Odzi Pilot Weekly Field Checklist

Before session
- Confirm participant list and consent status.
- Confirm devices, connectivity options, and backup paper forms.
- Prepare one-page recap of last week's findings for transparency.
- Confirm custodial anchor sign-off pair (operator + reviewer) is assigned.

During session
- Record attendance and farmer IDs.
- Observe one live record submission end-to-end.
- Ask trust survey questions and capture exact responses.
- Log incidents immediately (confusion, errors, trust concerns).

After session
- Reconcile submission counts with system logs.
- Update trust survey sheet and KPI tracker.
- Run receipt reproducibility spot-checks on sampled records.
- Complete daily anchor sign-off record with tx hash reference.
- Classify week as GO / ITERATE / NO-GO candidate.
- Capture top 3 blockers and assign owner + due date.

Hard stop escalation
- Any report of lost funds.
- Any unresolved allegation of data tampering.
- Any coercion or misleading claims about financing outcomes.
