Midnight Ecosystem Concept Note (Draft v1)

Working Title
Odzi Privacy Pilot: Real-World Validation of Midnight for Smallholder Agriculture

Author
EdgeChain team (Odzi, Zimbabwe)

Date
2026-03-21

==================================================
1) Executive Summary
==================================================

This proposal is not a DeFi lending product pitch.
It is a real-world, farmer-grounded validation program for Midnight.

We will:
- run a time-boxed pilot with a smallholder farming cooperative in Odzi, Zimbabwe;
- deploy a minimum Midnight interaction app focused on privacy-preserving records;
- measure real usage, trust, and operational behavior;
- present evidence-backed results and a funded roadmap for expansion.

Core thesis:
Midnight can demonstrate tangible adoption when privacy technology is applied to practical, low-friction workflows in communities that already value trust minimization and control over records.

==================================================
2) Context and Problem
==================================================

Smallholder farmers in Odzi operate in an environment where trust in formal systems is limited, records are fragmented, and proof of activity is difficult to standardize.

Current pain points observed in early discussions:
- paper-first records that are easy to lose or dispute;
- weak digital continuity of farm events (inputs, observations, deliveries);
- limited confidence that records are immutable and independently verifiable;
- low appetite for heavy technical onboarding unless immediate value is visible.

Important scope statement:
- We are not claiming to solve financing at this stage.
- We are validating the behavior and trust prerequisites required before any finance-linked use case.

==================================================
3) Why Midnight (and Why This Pilot)
==================================================

Midnight is uniquely suited to this stage because it enables:
- private user interactions with verifiable outcomes;
- selective disclosure models that fit agricultural cooperative workflows;
- trust-minimized record anchors without exposing sensitive participant details.

This pilot gives Midnight:
- a grounded, non-speculative use-case in a real underserved community;
- measurable field evidence beyond hackathon demos;
- reusable patterns for privacy-first adoption in emerging markets.

==================================================
4) What We Will Build (Minimum Midnight App)
==================================================

We will build a minimum workflow that farmers can use with support from field facilitators.

Feature set (strictly minimal):
1. Mobile-constrained trust model (phase-appropriate)
   - farmer mobile capture + deterministic receipts
   - daily initial custodial desktop root anchoring on Midnight
   - optional champion wallet lane on compatible devices
2. Record submission
   - farmer submits a simple field finding (structured form).
3. Verifiable receipt
   - each submission generates a proof-linked receipt identifier.
4. Personal history view
   - farmer and facilitator can see prior records and receipt statuses.
5. Selective disclosure control (basic)
   - farmer chooses what is shareable to cooperative officers.

Non-goals in this phase:
- no lending, no credit underwriting, no token speculation mechanics;
- no complex financial promises to participants.
- no mandatory per-farmer trustless wallet flow on low-cost Android in phase 1.
- no claim of non-custodial farmer-side wallet execution in phase 1.

==================================================
5) Pilot Design (Pre-Funding Traction Phase)
==================================================

Pilot size and duration:
- cohort: 20-30 farmers in one Odzi cooperative;
- duration: 6 weeks;
- format: weekly guided sessions + between-session usage.

Approach:
- week 0 baseline and consent;
- weeks 1-6 usage cycles, weekly trust survey, and KPI tracking;
- champion subgroup handles deeper wallet interaction tests.

Primary hypotheses:
H1 Trust: farmers perceive digital receipts as credible proof.
H2 Behavior: farmers repeatedly use the workflow without heavy hand-holding.
H3 Data discipline: records are complete and valid enough for future use-case expansion.
H4 Anchoring integrity in initial custodial lane: roots/proofs remain consistently verifiable.
H5 Wallet readiness: champion subgroup can onboard/sign reliably.

Pass thresholds (initial):
- activation >= 75% (week 1);
- week 4 retention >= 50%;
- week 6 retention >= 40%;
- completeness >= 90%;
- invalid submissions <= 5%;
- average trust >= 4.0/5;
- trust score 4 or 5 >= 70%;
- anchoring coverage >= 90%;
- inclusion proof check success >= 99%;
- wallet setup success (champions) >= 80%.

==================================================
6) Deliverables to Midnight
==================================================

At the end of the pilot, we will produce a Readiness Dossier:

1. Pilot outcomes report
   - KPI trends, threshold pass/fail, and decision analysis.
2. Farmer evidence pack
   - anonymized user journey snapshots and structured feedback.
3. Product learning memo
   - friction points, UX changes, and architecture implications.
4. Technical brief
   - Midnight integration details and operational constraints.
5. Scale plan
   - clear next-phase scope requiring ecosystem funding.

==================================================
7) Proposed Ask to Midnight (Post-Evidence)
==================================================

After pre-funding traction is demonstrated, we propose a funded 4-6 month expansion phase.

Funding objective:
- move from single-coop validation to multi-coop replication and stronger product instrumentation.

Indicative funded workstreams:
- product hardening and support tooling;
- cooperative onboarding kit and facilitator training;
- data quality and observability improvements;
- independent evaluation of trust/behavior outcomes;
- compliance and governance support for future expansion pathways.

Indicative budget framing (to refine with Midnight):
- Option A (lean): USD 40k-60k
- Option B (full validation): USD 80k-120k

We are open to milestone-based disbursement tied to clear KPI gates.

8) Why This Is a Strong Midnight Opportunity
==================================================

- Real users, real constraints, real behavior data.
- A non-hype use-case aligned to Midnight's privacy strengths.
- A credible path from pilot evidence to ecosystem-level learning.
- Reusability for other geographies and sectors with similar trust constraints.

==================================================
9) Risk Register and Mitigation
==================================================

Risk: wallet onboarding friction causes early drop-off.
Mitigation: champion-first rollout; facilitator-assisted onboarding; simplified flows.

Risk: pilot mistaken as financial promise.
Mitigation: clear consent language and non-financial positioning in all sessions.

Risk: weak data quality from manual errors.
Mitigation: strict field templates, weekly quality audits, guided form design.

Risk: regulatory ambiguity for future finance-linked expansion.
Mitigation: keep current phase non-financial; introduce legal/compliance track before any financial use-case stage.

==================================================

==================================================

2. run 6-week Odzi pilot and produce readiness dossier;
3. submit evidence-led proposal to Midnight for expansion support;
4. begin funded phase only against agreed milestones.
